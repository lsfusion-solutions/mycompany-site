Use pre-defined modules or customize them to your needs

For wholesale, retail, e-commerce, services, contracting, small-scale and custom production.

  • Procurement

    Handle the procurement process and control the document flow - from the creation of a request for quotation to suppliers to payment and placement of goods in warehouses.

  • Sales

    Product promotion, pricing policy management, configure discounts, implementation and receipt of payments.

  • Warehouse

    Manage all warehouses from one place. Addressable storage system, batch accounting, inventory control.

  • Production

    Automation of production processes. Control and disposal of used materials, registration of ready products.

  • Settlements

    Control of all company finances, terms of delivery, payment and receivables, quick registration of returns.

  • Services

    Registration and tracking of services, accounting for materials and time spent, planning and distribution of orders.

  • Cashier place

    The possibility to replace the cash register with an ordinary computer

Developer and Administrator-friendly

For developer For administrator
  • A completed medium-complexity ERP system in just 25K lines of code. Full-function IDE based on Intellij IDEA. Ideal for Agile development.
  • ACID right out of the box. No manual locks or transaction management. All constraints and events are global. High declarativeness.
  • One paradigm. One language. No need to know dozens of paradigms and languages, no need to choose between procedures and requests, client and server, forms and reports, OLTP and OLAP.
  • Open data storage structure. Transparent integration interfaces. Open source platform. Built on the most spread technologies in the world.
  • From CRUD to ERP. A nearly perfect modularity. Develop in parallel, reuse.
  • Разрешение на модификацию платформы.
  • Internationalize any line in one step.
