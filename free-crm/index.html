Questions, answered

Before you start

Need a detail we have not covered? Talk to our team.

Is MyCompany CRM really free?

Yes. MyCompany is open source under Apache 2.0, with no license fees, hidden tier or per-user limit. You deploy it on your own infrastructure.

Does the CRM include a kanban board?

Yes. Columns match your lead statuses, and moving a card updates the opportunity stage. Card order is remembered separately for each user.

Can it work with phone calls and emails?

Yes. Calls can create or attach to leads, and emails are sent from the lead card. Zadarma integration adds automatic call logging, number recognition, recordings, click-to-call and a WebRTC widget.

How does a lead become an order or invoice?

After document types are selected in CRM settings, create the sales order or invoice directly from the lead. Customer, address and expected revenue carry over automatically.

Is CRM connected to inventory and invoicing?

Yes. CRM is part of the MyCompany ERP, so leads, orders, invoices and stock share one transactional database rather than fragile integrations between separate products.

What do I need to run it?

A server with 4 GB RAM is enough to start. Install with one Linux command, the Windows installer or Docker; updates replace a single JAR file.
